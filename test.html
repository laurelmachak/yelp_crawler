        Specialties

        Jack's Prime burgers & shakes is putting a modern spin on the classic burger diner. Bring your friends and family and sit down to enjoy one of our classic flame-grilled burgers and be sure to wash it down with one of our hand spun shakes.

        At Jack's Prime burgers & shakes, we only use the finest local ingredients to give you a Californian twist on an American classic. You can easily find a great beer or wine to partner with your burger.

        It is our mission at Jack's Prime burgers & shakes to provide our guests with burgers made from the finest ingredients and to bring authentic hospitality to everyone who dines with us.

        History

        Established in 2008.

        After Jack's Prime burgers & shakes opened in 2008, those in our new community began to take notice of our burgers and hospitality. Since the day we opened it has been our mission to serve food made form only the finest ingredients in a family friendly environment. With more than 6 years of serving up delicious flame-grilled burgers and shakes, we hope that you bring your friends and family and sit down and dine with us.

        Meet the Business Owner

        Micheal M.
        Business Owner

        Mike had the dream to open a traditional American diner that served food made with only the finest local ingredients coupled with genuine hospitality. Mike enjoys serving up flame-grilled burgers and hand spun shakes in a family friendly environment. It is Mike's hope that you bring your friends and family into Jack's Prime burgers & shakes when you're looking for an American classic with a Californian twist.
